LoL World Championship 2022

World Championship 2022 is the twelfth of a series of final tournaments of the year for League of Legends. In the 2022 tournament, the best teams from all the leading official Riot Games leagues will compete for the title of the best team on the planet and for the prize fund of the tournament, which will be announced later. The final matches of the event will be held on basketball courts. One of them will be the Scotiabank Arena of the Toronto Raptors, and the other will be the Chase Center of the Golden State Warriors. The group stage and quarterfinals will be held at the Hulu Theatre at Madison Square Garden.
